# Contributing

Status: limited contribution model  
Last updated: July 2026

Decision Memo Template is maintained as a flagship product in Saurabh Chawda's Product Leadership Platform.

External contributions are intentionally limited at launch.

## What Is Welcome

- Documentation corrections.
- Broken link reports.
- Accessibility issues.
- Typo fixes.
- Suggestions that improve memo clarity, executive readability, templates, or examples.

## What Is Not Accepted

- Edits to Saurabh's personal career positioning.
- Changes to company-specific evidence or outcomes.
- Rewrites of Product OS positioning.
- New templates without prior discussion.
- Large architecture changes.

## Contribution Principle

Contributions should help Product Managers communicate decisions more clearly, briefly, and with stronger evidence.
