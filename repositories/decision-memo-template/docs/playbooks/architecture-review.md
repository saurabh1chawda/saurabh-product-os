# Architecture Review

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook when a product decision depends on platform, architecture, reliability, scalability, or integration trade-offs.

## Before the Review

- State the product outcome the architecture supports.
- Compare at least two credible implementation paths.
- Include customer, business, and engineering consequences.
- Identify reversibility and migration risk.

## Review Questions

- What product outcome depends on this architecture decision?
- What constraint are we solving first?
- Which option creates the most long-term leverage?
- What customer risk does each option introduce?
- What operational risk does each option reduce?
- What decision can be made now, and what remains unknown?

## Output

- Chosen architecture path.
- Product sequencing decision.
- Risks accepted.
- Follow-up validation or technical spike.
