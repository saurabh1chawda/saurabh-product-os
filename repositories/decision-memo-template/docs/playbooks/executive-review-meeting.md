# Executive Review Meeting

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook to run an executive review where the goal is a clear approve, reject, redirect, or defer decision.

## Before the Meeting

- Send the memo at least one business day before review.
- Put the recommendation and decision request at the top.
- Highlight the strongest evidence and biggest trade-off.
- Name the decision owner.
- Identify which question must be resolved live.

## Agenda

| Step | Time | Output |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Confirm decision request | 5 min | Shared understanding of the ask |
| Review recommendation | 10 min | Agreement on proposed path |
| Inspect evidence | 10 min | Evidence questions captured |
| Review trade-offs and risks | 10 min | Accepted / unresolved risks |
| Make decision | 10 min | Approve / reject / redirect / defer |

## Output

- Final decision.
- Owner.
- Follow-up actions.
- Review date.
- Memo update if the decision changes.
