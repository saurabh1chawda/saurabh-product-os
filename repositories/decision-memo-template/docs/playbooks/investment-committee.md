# Investment Committee

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook when the decision requires capacity, funding, executive sponsorship, or cross-functional commitment.

## Required Memo Sections

- Decision request.
- Recommendation.
- Business value.
- Customer value.
- Alternatives considered.
- Risks and mitigations.
- Success metrics.
- Confidence level.

## Committee Questions

- What investment is being requested?
- What customer or business outcome improves?
- What evidence supports the recommendation?
- What happens if we do nothing?
- What alternatives were rejected?
- What would make us stop or redirect?

## Output

- Approved / rejected / redirected investment.
- Funding or capacity decision.
- Success metrics.
- Review date.
