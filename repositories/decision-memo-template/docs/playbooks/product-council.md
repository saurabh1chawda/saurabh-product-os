# Product Council

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook to review product decisions that affect roadmap direction, customer experience, platform strategy, or cross-team dependencies.

## Before Council

- Share the memo.
- Identify the decision owner.
- Mark the confidence level.
- List the unresolved assumptions.
- Clarify whether the council is deciding, advising, or escalating.

## Council Agenda

| Step | Time | Output |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Decision framing | 5 min | Shared decision request |
| Evidence review | 10 min | Evidence questions |
| Stakeholder alignment | 10 min | Trade-offs named |
| Decision confidence | 5 min | Low / Medium / High |
| Final action | 10 min | Decide / validate / escalate |

## Output

- Council recommendation.
- Final owner.
- Next action.
- Memo update.
