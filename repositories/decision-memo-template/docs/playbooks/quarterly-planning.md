# Quarterly Planning

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook to turn roadmap debates into written prioritization decisions.

## Before Planning

- Prepare Roadmap Prioritization Memos for major trade-offs.
- Group decisions by customer outcome, business outcome, and capacity constraint.
- Identify which work should stop, continue, or move later.

## Planning Flow

| Step | Output |
| --- | --- |
| Review strategic objectives | Planning criteria |
| Review customer and business evidence | Evidence inventory |
| Compare roadmap options | Prioritization memo |
| Make sequencing decisions | Roadmap decision log |
| Assign review checkpoints | Measurement plan |

## Output

- Prioritized roadmap.
- Deferred work list.
- Explicit trade-offs.
- Decision owners.
- Review dates.
