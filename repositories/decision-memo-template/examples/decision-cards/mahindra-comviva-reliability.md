# Decision Card: Mahindra Comviva Reliability Before Expansion

Status: Production-quality decision card  
Related worked example: [Mahindra Comviva Worked Example](../mahindra-comviva.md)

## Decision

Prioritize reliability, transaction confidence, and customer trust before accelerating platform expansion.

## Context

The product operated in mobile money and payment workflows where reliability directly affected customer confidence, enterprise adoption, and operational risk.

## Recommendation

Strengthen core transaction workflows and trust signals before expanding platform capability.

## Alternatives

| Alternative | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| Expand platform features immediately | Rejected |
| Run narrow stabilization only | Rejected |
| Reliability-first sequencing | Chosen |

## Evidence

- Transaction-scale product environment.
- Enterprise customer trust requirements.
- 10M+ monthly transactions supported.
- 94% customer satisfaction context.

## Trade-off

Accept slower visible feature expansion to protect customer confidence and long-term platform adoption.

## Confidence

High.

Reason: payment workflows carry high customer and business stakes, and reliability is a prerequisite for adoption.

## Outcome

Reliability-first sequencing supported transaction-scale product experiences across mobile money and payment systems.
