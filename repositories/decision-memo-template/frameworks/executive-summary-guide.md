# Executive Summary Guide

Status: v1 framework  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

The executive summary should make the decision legible before the reader enters the details.

## Recommended Structure

### 1. Decision Request

What decision do you need from the reader?

### 2. Recommendation

What do you recommend?

### 3. Rationale

Why is this the best path?

### 4. Evidence

What evidence matters most?

### 5. Trade-off

What are we accepting or delaying?

### 6. Next Step

What happens after approval?

## Writing Rules

- Keep it under 150 words for most decisions.
- Use plain language.
- Avoid internal shorthand.
- Include the recommendation in the first sentence when possible.
- Do not use the summary to recap every detail.

## Test

If someone reads only the executive summary, they should understand:

- The decision requested
- The recommendation
- The main evidence
- The key trade-off
- The next action
