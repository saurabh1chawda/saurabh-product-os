# AI Opportunity Canvas

Status: v1 template  
Last updated: July 2026

Use this canvas to define an AI opportunity before scoring it.

## 1. Opportunity Summary

- Opportunity name:
- Product area:
- Customer segment:
- Decision owner:
- Review date:

## 2. Customer Workflow

- Who is the user?
- What workflow are they completing?
- Where does friction appear?
- How often does this happen?
- What is the cost of failure?

## 3. Customer Value

- What customer problem would become easier?
- What behavior would change?
- What existing workaround does the customer use?
- Why would AI improve the workflow?

## 4. Business Value

- Which business outcome could improve?
- How would success be measured?
- What is the expected strategic value?
- Does this create differentiation or platform leverage?

## 5. Execution Readiness

- What data is required?
- Is the data usable?
- Can model quality be evaluated?
- What operational support is needed?
- What is the cost profile?

## 6. Trust and Risk

- What can go wrong?
- Who is accountable for the decision?
- Can humans override the system?
- What fallback path exists?
- Is auditability required?

## 7. Recommendation

- Build Now
- Prototype
- Validate Further
- Park
- Reject

## 8. Next Step

Write the next action that should happen before investment increases.
