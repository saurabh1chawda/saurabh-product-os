## Pre-Work

- Define the opportunity.
- Bring customer evidence.
- Bring business objective.
- Bring data readiness notes.
- Bring risk and trust considerations.

## Agenda

### 1. Frame the Opportunity

Time: 10 minutes

Output: One clear opportunity statement.

### 2. Review Customer Workflow

Time: 15 minutes

Output: Workflow friction, frequency, and customer value.

### 3. Review Business Value

Time: 10 minutes

Output: Business outcome and measurement approach.

### 4. Review Execution Readiness

Time: 15 minutes

Output: Data, model, engineering, and operational readiness.

### 5. Review Trust and Risk

Time: 15 minutes

Output: Required safeguards, human control, fallback, and auditability.

### 6. Score and Decide

Time: 15 minutes

Output: Build Now, Prototype, Validate Further, Park, or Reject.
