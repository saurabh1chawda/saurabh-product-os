Status: Framework application placeholder  
Related context: Workflow adoption before content expansion

## Opportunity Framing

The product opportunity was not simply to add richer content or more intelligent content generation. The higher-leverage question was whether the product could help researchers complete real scientific workflows more successfully.

## Framework Application

Strong signals:

- Customer workflow pain was meaningful.
- Workflow adoption mattered more than content volume.
- Product value depended on behavior change.

Risks to evaluate:

- Whether AI would improve workflow execution or only create content novelty.
- Whether users would trust guidance inside scientific workflows.
- Whether success could be measured through engagement and adoption quality.

## Likely Recommendation

Validate Further or Prototype.

The opportunity should be prioritized only if AI improves workflow completion, not if it merely increases content production.
