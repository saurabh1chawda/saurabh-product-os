# Simplilearn Example

Status: Framework application placeholder  
Related context: Growth experimentation and funnel optimization

## Opportunity Framing

AI should be prioritized only if it improves conversion, learner decision quality, personalization, or funnel efficiency beyond simpler experimentation and UX improvements.

## Framework Application

Strong signals:

- Business value can be measured through conversion and revenue.
- Customer behavior change is observable.
- Experimentation can validate impact before scaling.

Risks to evaluate:

- Whether AI adds complexity without improving conversion.
- Whether simpler funnel optimization would outperform AI.
- Whether success metrics capture durable customer value, not just engagement.

## Likely Recommendation

Prototype or Validate Further.

AI should be considered only after identifying the highest-leverage conversion constraint.
