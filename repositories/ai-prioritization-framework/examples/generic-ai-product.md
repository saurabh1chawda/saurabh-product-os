# Generic AI Product Example

Status: Reusable example placeholder

## Opportunity

An enterprise SaaS team wants to add an AI assistant that recommends next actions for account managers.

## Evaluation

| Dimension | Signal |
| --- | --- |
| Customer Value | Account managers spend time synthesizing scattered customer information |
| Business Value | Better next actions could improve retention and expansion |
| Execution Readiness | Data exists but quality and permissions are uneven |
| Trust & Risk | Recommendations affect customer relationships and require human review |

## Anti-Pattern Check

- Rules may handle some recommendations.
- Human approval is mandatory.
- Trust depends on explainability and source visibility.

## Recommendation

Prototype.

Start with AI-assisted recommendations and human approval. Do not launch autonomous actions until trust, data quality, and measurable business impact are proven.

## Next Step

Run a limited workflow pilot with clear metrics:

- Recommendation acceptance rate
- Time saved
- Account manager trust signal
- Customer outcome influenced
- Override rate
