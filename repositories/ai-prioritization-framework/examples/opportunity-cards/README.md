# AI Opportunity Cards

Status: v1 card format  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Opportunity cards summarize one AI opportunity in a format that can be used in roadmap workshops, AI investment reviews, and executive product discussions.

Cards should be short enough to compare quickly but specific enough to support a real decision.

## Card Format

Each opportunity card should include:

- Opportunity name
- Customer problem
- Proposed AI capability
- Customer value
- Business value
- Execution readiness
- Trust and risk
- Score
- Recommendation
- Next step
- Related evidence

## Quality Bar

An opportunity card should help a team decide whether the opportunity deserves more investment, not simply describe the idea.
