# Opportunity Card: Logix AI-Ready Platform

Status: Production-quality opportunity card  
Related worked example: [Logix Worked Example](../logix.md)

## Opportunity

Build an AI-ready platform foundation before expanding advanced customer-facing AI capabilities.

## Customer Problem

Customers needed faster, simpler, more reliable digital workflows, but the existing platform was constrained by technical debt, slow release cycles, fragmented journeys, and desktop-first experiences.

## Proposed AI Capability

Near term: AI-ready platform foundation and constrained AI-readiness pilots.  
Later: customer-facing AI capabilities once platform readiness, measurement, and trust safeguards improve.

## Customer Value

Score: 4.0 / 5

Why it matters:

- Improves reliability and customer workflow quality.
- Enables faster product improvements.
- Reduces friction from legacy platform constraints.

## Business Value

Score: 4.7 / 5

Why it matters:

- Supports ARR, MRR growth, engagement, and release velocity.
- Builds long-term platform leverage.
- Makes future AI investment more commercially durable.

## Execution Readiness

Score: 2.8 / 5

Why it matters:

- Platform modernization is required before broad AI expansion.
- Technical debt and operational complexity reduce immediate readiness.

## Trust & Risk

Score: 3.0 / 5

Why it matters:

- Reliability and user trust must improve before deeper automation.
- Future AI features need observability, fallback paths, and confidence signals.

## Final Score

74 / 100

## Recommendation

Prototype.

Fund the AI-ready platform foundation and run constrained AI-readiness pilots. Do not prioritize broad customer-facing AI features yet.

## Next Step

Define the first AI-readiness pilot tied to one customer workflow and one measurable platform capability.
