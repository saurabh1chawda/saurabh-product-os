# First Discovery Sprint

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook to run a one-week discovery sprint around a new opportunity.

## Sprint Goal

Move from unclear problem signal to a decision about whether to prioritize, prototype, validate further, reframe, or stop.

## Day-by-Day Plan

| Day | Focus | Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Frame opportunity and assumptions | Discovery Canvas and Assumption Map |
| Day 2 | Interview customers and stakeholders | Interview notes and evidence inventory |
| Day 3 | Observe workflow and collect data | Workflow notes and evidence ladder placement |
| Day 4 | Synthesize evidence | Opportunity Assessment and Validation Scorecard |
| Day 5 | Make recommendation | Decision and next-step plan |

## Required Artifacts

- Discovery Canvas
- Customer Interview Guide
- Assumption Mapping
- Validation Scorecard

## Decision Output

Choose one:

- Prioritize
- Prototype
- Validate further
- Reframe
- Stop
