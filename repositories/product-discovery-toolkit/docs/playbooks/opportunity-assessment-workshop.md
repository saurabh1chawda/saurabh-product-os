# Opportunity Assessment Workshop

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook to evaluate whether an opportunity deserves roadmap investment.

## Participants

- Product
- Design
- Research
- Engineering
- Data
- Customer-facing stakeholder
- Decision owner

## Agenda

| Step | Time | Output |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Frame opportunity | 10 min | Shared problem statement |
| Review customer workflow | 15 min | Workflow map |
| Review evidence | 20 min | Evidence quality assessment |
| Map assumptions | 15 min | Riskiest assumption |
| Score readiness | 15 min | Validation Scorecard |
| Decide next step | 15 min | Prioritize / Prototype / Validate / Reframe / Stop |

## Required Artifacts

- Discovery Canvas
- Evidence Ladder
- Opportunity Assessment
- Validation Scorecard

## Decision Rule

Do not prioritize if the team cannot explain who has the problem, what workflow is affected, what evidence supports it, and what decision would change based on new evidence.
