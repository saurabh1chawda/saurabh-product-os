# Weekly Discovery Review

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook to maintain discovery quality during ongoing product work.

## Review Questions

- What did we learn this week?
- Which assumption changed?
- Which customer behavior did we observe?
- What evidence moved up the Evidence Ladder?
- What should we stop believing?
- What decision is now clearer?
- What should we do next?

## Review Agenda

| Step | Time | Output |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Review evidence | 10 min | New evidence inventory |
| Update assumptions | 10 min | Supported / rejected / unknown list |
| Review opportunity readiness | 10 min | Readiness status |
| Decide next action | 10 min | Next discovery or roadmap decision |

## Output

- Updated Discovery Canvas.
- Updated Assumption Map.
- Decision log entry.
- Next week's discovery focus.
