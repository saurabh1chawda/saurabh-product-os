# Customer Interview Session

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook to run a customer interview that produces decision-quality evidence.

## Before the Session

- Define the decision this interview informs.
- Identify the riskiest assumption.
- Review the Customer Interview Guide.
- Prepare workflow reconstruction prompts.
- Avoid showing the solution too early.

## During the Session

1. Establish context.
2. Reconstruct the last real workflow.
3. Probe pain, frequency, and cost of failure.
4. Capture workarounds.
5. Ask decision and trust questions.
6. Confirm what evidence would make the customer change behavior.

## After the Session

- Record direct quotes.
- Separate stated intent from observed behavior.
- Update the Evidence Ladder.
- Mark assumptions as supported, rejected, or unknown.
- Decide whether follow-up is needed.

## Output

- Interview notes.
- Evidence inventory.
- Assumption updates.
- Recommended next step.
