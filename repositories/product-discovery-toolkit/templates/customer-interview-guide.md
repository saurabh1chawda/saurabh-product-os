## When to Use

Use this template when the team needs to learn:

- Whether a customer problem exists.
- How the workflow works today.
- Which assumptions are risky.
- Whether the opportunity deserves more investment.

## Opening Script

Thank you for taking the time to speak with us. The goal today is to understand how you work today, not to sell or validate a specific solution. There are no right answers. We are most interested in your actual workflow, recent examples, workarounds, and what makes the problem important or unimportant.

## Warm-Up

- Tell me about your role and what you are responsible for.
- How often do you deal with this workflow?
- What tools or people are involved?

## Workflow Reconstruction

- Walk me through the last time you handled this workflow.
- What happened immediately before this step?
- What happened immediately after?
- Where did you lose time, confidence, context, or momentum?
- What did you do outside the product or system?
- Who else was involved?

## Problem Exploration

- What makes this problem important?
- How often does it happen?
- What happens if it goes unresolved?
- What is the cost of delay, error, or rework?
- What do you do today to work around it?
- What have you tried that did not work?

## Decision and Buying Context

- When did you first realize this was a problem?
- What would make you change your current approach?
- Who would need to trust or approve a new solution?
- How would you know a better solution worked?
- What would make this not worth changing?
