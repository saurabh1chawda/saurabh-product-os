## Opening

Thank the participant, explain the purpose, and clarify that the goal is to learn about their workflow rather than sell or validate a specific solution.

## Workflow Questions

- Walk me through the last time you handled this workflow.
- What happened before this step?
- What happened after it?
- Which parts were easy?
- Which parts were frustrating?
- Where did you lose time, confidence, or context?

## Problem Questions

- What makes this problem important?
- How often does it happen?
- What happens if it goes unresolved?
- Who else is affected?
- What do you do today to work around it?

## Decision Questions

- When did you first realize this was a problem?
- What alternatives have you tried?
- What would make you change your current approach?
- How would you know a better solution worked?
