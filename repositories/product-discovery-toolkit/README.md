# Product Discovery Toolkit

Status: v1 repository foundation  
Last updated: July 2026  
Product Family: Product Discovery  
Product Collection: Product Toolkit Collection

Product Discovery Toolkit helps Product Managers validate customer problems before committing product, engineering, design, or go-to-market investment.

It provides reusable frameworks, templates, examples, and workshop-ready artifacts for turning discovery from informal conversations into repeatable product decision practice.

## Executive Summary

Good discovery reduces the cost of being wrong.

This toolkit helps teams understand customer workflows, identify meaningful problems, map assumptions, evaluate evidence quality, and decide whether an opportunity is ready for solution investment.

The goal is simple: gather better evidence before making product decisions.

## Problem Statement

Teams often move from customer requests, stakeholder opinions, sales pressure, or competitor activity directly into roadmap commitments.

Without structured discovery, teams risk:

- Solving symptoms instead of root problems.
- Mistaking requests for needs.
- Overbuilding before evidence exists.
- Prioritizing loud stakeholders over customer behavior.
- Treating validation as confirmation.
- Scaling solutions before the opportunity is understood.

## Why This Toolkit Exists

Discovery should help teams make better decisions, not simply collect more notes.

This repository exists to give Product Managers practical artifacts they can use immediately in customer interviews, problem framing, assumption mapping, opportunity assessment, and validation planning.

## Toolkit Overview

The toolkit is organized around five discovery jobs:

1. Understand the customer workflow.
2. Separate requests from underlying needs.
3. Map assumptions and evidence gaps.
4. Assess opportunity readiness.
5. Decide whether to build, test, reframe, or stop.
