## Discovery Influence

Discovery should clarify whether customer friction comes from visible product gaps, platform constraints, performance issues, mobile usability, or workflow reliability.

For platform products, discovery must include both customer workflow evidence and internal operating constraints.

Platform modernization decisions should be grounded in customer friction, business impact, and delivery constraints rather than engineering preference alone.
