# Generic Product Example

Status: Reusable example placeholder

## Scenario

A B2B SaaS team receives repeated requests for an AI summary feature.

## Discovery Question

What customer workflow is failing today, and would summaries actually improve the outcome?

## Discovery Plan

1. Interview customers about the current workflow.
2. Observe how they synthesize information today.
3. Map assumptions about frequency, pain, trust, and business value.
4. Run a prototype test with sample summaries.
5. Measure whether users make faster or better decisions.

## Possible Finding

Customers may not need summaries. They may need better prioritization, source visibility, workflow routing, or decision support.

## Recommended Decision

Validate further before roadmap commitment.
