# Discovery Card: JoVE Workflow Adoption

Status: Production-quality discovery card  
Related worked example: [JoVE Worked Example](../jove.md)

## Opportunity

Improve adoption by helping users integrate scientific content into teaching, learning, and research workflows.

## Customer Problem

Users valued scientific content, but adoption depended on whether the product helped them complete the workflow around that content.

## Initial Assumption

More scientific content would increase engagement and institutional value.

## Strongest Evidence

Workflow signals showed that users needed better support around syllabus mapping, content discovery in context, and practical workflow completion.

## Assumptions Validated

- Scientific content was valuable.
- Discovery mattered.
- Engagement influenced institutional value.

## Assumptions Rejected

- More content alone would solve adoption.
- Users would adapt their workflow around the product.
- Richer video features were the highest-leverage next investment.

## Opportunity Assessment

| Dimension | Assessment |
| --- | --- |
| Customer pain | High |
| Workflow frequency | High |
| Business impact | High |
| Evidence quality | Strong |
| Readiness | Ready for product strategy |

## Recommendation

Prioritize workflow adoption over content expansion.

## Next Step

Define roadmap investments that help users map, discover, and apply content inside real workflows.
