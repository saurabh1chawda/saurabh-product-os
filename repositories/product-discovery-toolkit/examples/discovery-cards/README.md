# Discovery Cards

Status: v1 card format  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Discovery cards summarize one opportunity in a format that can be used in discovery reviews, roadmap planning, and executive discussions.

## Card Format

Each discovery card should include:

- Opportunity name
- Customer problem
- Initial assumption
- Strongest evidence
- Assumptions validated
- Assumptions rejected
- Opportunity assessment
- Recommendation
- Next step
- Related evidence

## Quality Bar

A discovery card should help a team decide what to do next, not merely summarize research activity.
