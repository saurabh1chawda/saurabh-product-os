# Simplilearn Example

Status: Discovery application placeholder  
Related context: Growth experimentation and funnel optimization

## Discovery Influence

Discovery should identify the highest-leverage constraint in the funnel before scaling acquisition or adding new programs.

The discovery question is not "How do we get more traffic?" It is "Where does customer intent fail to become customer action?"

## Discovery Artifacts to Use

- Opportunity Assessment
- Experiment Canvas
- Validation Scorecard
- Assumption Mapping

## Discovery Lesson

Growth discovery should connect customer behavior, funnel friction, experiment evidence, and business outcomes before increasing spend.
