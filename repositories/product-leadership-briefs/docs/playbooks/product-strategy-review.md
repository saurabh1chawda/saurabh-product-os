# Product Strategy Review

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook to connect product strategy decisions to customer behavior, business outcomes, and reusable leadership principles.

## Agenda

| Step | Output |
| --- | --- |
| Review strategic question | Clear decision request |
| Compare relevant briefs | Decision patterns |
| Score options | Brief Scorecard or decision matrix |
| Discuss trade-offs | Explicit choices |
| Extract principle | Reusable product lesson |

## Output

- Strategy recommendation.
- Decision rationale.
- Reusable principle.
- Follow-up evidence needed.
