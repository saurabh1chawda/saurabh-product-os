# Growth Review

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook to review whether growth decisions are improving the system or merely increasing demand.

## Suggested Brief

- Simplilearn

## Review Questions

- Is traffic the constraint or is conversion the constraint?
- Where does the funnel leak?
- Which experiment has the highest learning value?
- Are we optimizing before scaling?
- What metric proves durable growth?

## Output

- Growth bottleneck.
- Experiment sequence.
- Metrics and review date.
- Scale / optimize / stop decision.
