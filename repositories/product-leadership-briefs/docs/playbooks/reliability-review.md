# Reliability Review

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook to evaluate product decisions where reliability, trust, compliance, or transaction confidence affect adoption.

## Suggested Brief

- Mahindra Comviva

## Review Questions

- What customer workflow depends on reliability?
- What is the cost of failure?
- Which trust signal is measurable?
- What expansion should wait until reliability improves?
- How will confidence be communicated to customers and stakeholders?

## Output

- Reliability decision.
- Trust metrics.
- Expansion readiness criteria.
- Follow-up review date.
