# Platform Modernization Decision

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook when a product decision requires platform investment, architecture sequencing, reliability work, or AI readiness.

## Suggested Briefs

- Logix
- Mahindra Comviva

## Review Questions

- What customer or business constraint is the platform creating?
- What happens if we keep patching?
- What happens if we rewrite too much?
- Which modernization path preserves customer value?
- How does this decision improve future product leverage?

## Output

- Modernization recommendation.
- Sequencing plan.
- Risk trade-offs.
- Platform success metrics.
