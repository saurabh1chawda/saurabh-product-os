# Executive Roadmap Review

Status: v1 playbook  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Use this playbook to review whether roadmap decisions are grounded in customer value, business leverage, evidence, and explicit trade-offs.

## Preparation

- Select 2 to 3 Product Leadership Briefs relevant to the roadmap decision.
- Summarize decision patterns and reusable principles.
- Identify which current roadmap trade-off resembles past decisions.

## Review Questions

- What is the highest-leverage constraint?
- Which option creates durable customer value?
- What evidence supports the recommendation?
- What are we choosing not to do?
- What metric will prove the decision worked?

## Output

- Roadmap decision.
- Accepted trade-offs.
- Evidence gaps.
- Owner and review date.
