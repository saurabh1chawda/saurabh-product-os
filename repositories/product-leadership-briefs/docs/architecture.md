# Architecture

Status: v1 foundation  
Last updated: July 2026

## System Overview

Product Leadership Briefs is a documentation-first product repository. It organizes product decisions into reusable executive briefs supported by a standard decision framework, template, decision index, and governance model.

The repository should help visitors understand how product judgment can be documented clearly without reading a long portfolio story.

## Folder Responsibilities

| Folder | Responsibility |
| --- | --- |
| `briefs/` | Brief navigation, expanded flagship briefs, and decision index |
| `frameworks/` | Product Decision Framework, Brief Scorecard, Decision Reusability, and reusable decision models |
| `templates/` | Leadership Brief template and future brief variants |
| `assets/` | Public screenshots, exports, and supporting assets |
| `diagrams/` | Mermaid visual models that clarify product decisions |
| `docs/` | Architecture, governance, roadmap, product specification, downloads, and playbooks |

## Content Model

Each full Product Leadership Brief should include:

- Executive snapshot
- Context
- Problem
- Options considered
- Decision
- Trade-offs
- Stakeholder alignment
- Results
- Lessons learned
- Reusable principle
- Related Product OS evidence

## Relationship to Product OS

Product OS renders the full executive experience. This repository provides the reusable method, template, and index behind that experience.

Do not duplicate full Product OS pages. Use concise brief stubs and link to Product OS for complete evidence.
