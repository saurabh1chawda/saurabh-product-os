# Downloads

Status: v1 download guidance  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

Product Leadership Briefs should be easy to use in coaching, interview preparation, product strategy workshops, and executive review.

The repository is Markdown-first. Portable formats should preserve decision structure without turning briefs into generic case studies.

## Current Source Formats

| Artifact | Source Format | Location |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Flagship briefs | Markdown | `briefs/*.md` |
| Leadership cards | Markdown | `examples/leadership-cards/*.md` |
| Brief Scorecard | Markdown | `frameworks/brief-scorecard.md` |
| Decision Reusability | Markdown | `frameworks/decision-reusability.md` |
| Diagrams | Mermaid | `diagrams/*.mmd` |

## Supported Export Targets

### Markdown

Primary source of truth. Best for GitHub, internal decision libraries, and version-controlled learning artifacts.

### PDF

Recommended for interview preparation packets, executive review packets, and read-only brief sharing.

### Google Docs

Recommended for collaborative review, coaching comments, and stakeholder feedback.

### PowerPoint

Recommended for executive readouts. Keep one decision per slide and preserve the decision, options, trade-offs, and outcome.

### Notion

Recommended for internal product learning systems and team knowledge bases.

### Executive Brief

Future target for one-page leadership summaries that combine the brief, card, and scorecard.

## Export Standards

- Keep decision before narrative.
- Preserve metrics and trade-offs.
- Link to the Product OS evidence page when available.
- Include reusable principle and reusability level.
- Avoid adding unsupported claims when exporting.
