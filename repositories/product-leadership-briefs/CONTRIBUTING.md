# Contributing

Status: limited contribution model  
Last updated: July 2026

Product Leadership Briefs is maintained as a flagship product in Saurabh Chawda's Product Leadership Platform.

External contributions are intentionally limited at launch.

## What Is Welcome

- Documentation corrections.
- Broken link reports.
- Accessibility issues.
- Typo fixes.
- Suggestions that improve the reusable brief framework, templates, decision index, or teaching structure.

## What Is Not Accepted

- Edits to Saurabh's personal career positioning.
- Changes to company-specific metrics or Product Leadership Brief evidence.
- Rewrites of Product OS positioning.
- New brief narratives without prior discussion.
- Large architecture changes.

## Contribution Principle

Contributions should make product decision documentation clearer, more reusable, or easier to learn from without changing the source evidence.
