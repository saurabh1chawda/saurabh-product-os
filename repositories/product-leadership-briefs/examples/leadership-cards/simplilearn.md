# Leadership Card: Simplilearn

Status: v1 leadership card  
Related brief: [Simplilearn](../../briefs/simplilearn.md)

## Problem

Traffic was growing, but the conversion system leaked value and made additional acquisition less efficient.

## Decision

Optimize the funnel before scaling growth.

## Outcome

Improved revenue, conversion, referral contribution, and roadmap efficiency.

## Metrics

- 10x revenue growth.
- 62% traffic-to-lead.
- 40% lead-to-customer.
- Referral contribution improved from 1.5% to 3%.

## Reusable Principle

Optimization before scale.
