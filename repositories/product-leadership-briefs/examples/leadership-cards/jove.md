# Leadership Card: JoVE

Status: v1 leadership card  
Related brief: [JoVE](../../briefs/jove.md)

## Problem

Valuable scientific content was not enough if users could not apply it inside teaching, research, and laboratory workflows.

## Decision

Prioritize workflow adoption over content expansion.

## Outcome

Improved adoption and engagement by shifting from content volume to workflow value.

## Metrics

- +30% portfolio revenue.
- +40% session duration.
- +15% page views.
- +12% video consumption.

## Reusable Principle

Adoption before expansion.
