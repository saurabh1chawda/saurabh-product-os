# Leadership Card: Logix

Status: v1 leadership card  
Related brief: [Logix](../../briefs/logix.md)

## Problem

AI demand was increasing, but platform constraints, technical debt, and desktop-first experiences limited scalable execution.

## Decision

Modernize the platform before teaching it to think.

## Outcome

Improved commercial, customer, and platform readiness while creating a stronger foundation for future AI capability.

## Metrics

- Rs. 1M+ ARR.
- +20% MRR growth.
- +25% engagement.
- 3x delivery velocity.
- 2x faster releases.

## Reusable Principle

Platform before intelligence.
