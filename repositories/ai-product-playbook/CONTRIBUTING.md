# Contributing

Status: limited contribution model  
Last updated: July 2026

AI Product Playbook is maintained as a flagship product in Saurabh Chawda's Product Leadership Platform.

External contributions are intentionally limited at launch.

## What Is Welcome

- Documentation corrections.
- Broken link reports.
- Accessibility issues.
- Typo fixes.
- Suggestions that improve clarity of reusable AI product frameworks, templates, examples, or learning paths.

## What Is Not Accepted

- Edits to Saurabh's personal career positioning.
- Changes to company-specific evidence or Product Leadership Brief outcomes.
- Rewrites of Product OS positioning.
- New frameworks without prior discussion.
- Large architecture changes.

## Contribution Principle

Contributions should make the playbook clearer, more practical, or easier to reuse without turning this repository into a duplicate of Product OS.
