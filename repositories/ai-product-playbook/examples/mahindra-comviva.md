# Mahindra Comviva

## Context

Mahindra Comviva represents a payments and transaction-scale context where reliability, trust, and customer confidence mattered more than visible feature expansion.

## Product Decision

Improve platform reliability before expanding the product surface.

## Frameworks Applied

- Trust Before Automation
- AI Success Metrics
- Build vs Buy AI

## What This Teaches

Trust is a product requirement, not a polish layer. In high-risk workflows, users need reliability, control, auditability, and recovery paths before they accept more advanced automation.
