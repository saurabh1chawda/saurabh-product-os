# Logix

## Context

Logix represents an AI-ready platform modernization context where the product decision was to strengthen the platform foundation before expanding advanced AI capabilities.

## Product Decision

Modernize the platform before teaching it to think.

## Frameworks Applied

- Build vs Buy AI
- Trust Before Automation
- AI Success Metrics
- RAG vs Agent

## What This Teaches

AI leverage depends on platform leverage. If the underlying system cannot support reliable execution, measurement, cost control, and workflow integration, AI features can amplify existing weaknesses.
