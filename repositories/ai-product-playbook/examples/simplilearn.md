# Simplilearn

## Context

Simplilearn represents a growth and experimentation context where improving the conversion system created more leverage than increasing acquisition spend.

## Product Decision

Optimize the funnel before scaling growth.

## Frameworks Applied

- AI Success Metrics
- Opportunity Scoring
- AI Experiment Canvas

## What This Teaches

AI product metrics should connect to customer behavior and business outcomes. Measuring activity without understanding conversion, trust, cost, and retention can lead teams to scale the wrong system.
