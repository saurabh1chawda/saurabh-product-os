# Examples

Status: v1 example structure  
Last updated: July 2026

These examples show how AI Product Playbook thinking maps to real product contexts without duplicating Product Leadership Briefs.

Each example should explain which frameworks apply, what product decision they support, and what reusable lesson a Product Manager can take away.

| Example | Focus | Related Frameworks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Logix](logix.md) | AI-ready platform modernization | Build vs Buy AI, Trust Before Automation, AI Success Metrics |
| [JoVE](jove.md) | Workflow adoption before content expansion | Opportunity Scoring, Workflow to Agent, AI Prioritization |
| [Mahindra Comviva](mahindra-comviva.md) | Payment reliability and trust | Trust Before Automation, AI Success Metrics, Build vs Buy AI |
| [Simplilearn](simplilearn.md) | Growth experimentation and funnel optimization | AI Success Metrics, Opportunity Scoring, AI Experiment Canvas |

## Example Standard

Each example should include:

- Context
- Product decision
- Frameworks applied
- What this teaches
- Related Product OS evidence

The examples should remain concise. Product Leadership Briefs remain the deeper narrative source.
