# JoVE

## Context

JoVE represents a workflow adoption context where richer content was less important than helping users complete their scientific workflows.

## Product Decision

Prioritize workflow adoption over content expansion.

## Frameworks Applied

- Opportunity Scoring
- Workflow to Agent
- AI Prioritization
- AI Success Metrics

## What This Teaches

AI product opportunities should start from workflow friction. If users do not adopt the workflow, intelligence added on top of the product will not create durable value.
