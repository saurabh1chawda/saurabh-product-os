# Downloads

Status: v1 download guidance  
Last updated: July 2026

## Purpose

The AI Product Playbook should be portable for self-study, workshops, executive reviews, and team operating-system discussions.

The repository remains Markdown-first. Export formats should preserve navigation, lifecycle flow, and repository handoffs.

## Supported Export Targets

### PDF

Recommended for self-study, recruiter review, and executive sharing.

### PowerPoint

Recommended for leadership reviews and AI product workshops. Use one lifecycle stage or decision model per slide.

### Workshop Guide

Recommended for facilitated team sessions. Should include agenda, stage goals, artifacts, and decision outputs.

### Facilitator Guide

Recommended for product leaders or coaches running AI product reviews.

### Self-study Checklist

Recommended for individual Product Managers learning the ecosystem.

### Notion Index

Recommended for teams organizing the Playbook as an internal knowledge base.

## Export Standards

- Preserve repository links instead of copying detailed framework content.
- Keep lifecycle stages visible.
- Show expected outputs for each stage.
- Include Product OS as the executive overview.
- Use diagrams for navigation, not decoration.
