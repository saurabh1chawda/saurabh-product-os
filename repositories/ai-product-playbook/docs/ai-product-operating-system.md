## Purpose

The AI Product Operating System is a repeatable model for building AI products through evidence, workflow fit, trust, measurement, and business value.

It is not a process for adding AI to every roadmap. It is a decision model for determining when AI should exist, how it should behave, and when it is ready to scale.

## Stage Guide

| Stage | Objective | Deliverables | Associated Repositories |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Observe | Notice customer behavior, workflow friction, market signals, and business constraints | Problem signal, workflow observation, assumption list | Product Discovery Toolkit |
| Discover | Understand the customer problem and evidence quality | Interview notes, discovery canvas, evidence ladder | Product Discovery Toolkit |
| Prioritize | Decide whether the opportunity deserves AI investment | Opportunity score, prioritization recommendation | AI Prioritization Framework |
| Prototype | Test the riskiest workflow, trust, and feasibility assumptions | Prototype plan, test scope, learning goals | AI Product Playbook |
| Validate | Confirm customer behavior change and workflow improvement | Validation scorecard, experiment results | Product Discovery Toolkit |
| Decide | Make the investment, architecture, or sequencing decision explicit | Executive decision memo, alternatives, risks | Decision Memo Template |
| Execute | Build the customer experience, trust model, evaluation loop, and operating model | Roadmap, launch plan, safeguards, owners | AI Product Playbook |
| Measure | Connect AI performance to workflow, customer, and business outcomes | Metrics model, evaluation plan, review cadence | AI Product Playbook |
| Scale | Expand only when trust, economics, reliability, and adoption are strong | Scale decision, platform plan, governance | Product Leadership Briefs |
| Learn | Extract reusable product judgment from outcomes | Leadership brief, reusable principle, retrospective | Product Leadership Briefs |

## Operating Principle

Architectures should become more intelligent only when customer value requires it.

## How This Connects the Platform

The Playbook coordinates the operating system. The other flagship repositories provide the detailed artifacts.

- Product Discovery Toolkit owns discovery quality.
- AI Prioritization Framework owns investment quality.
- Decision Memo Template owns executive communication.
- Product Leadership Briefs owns decision evidence and reusable lessons.
- Product OS owns the public executive narrative.
