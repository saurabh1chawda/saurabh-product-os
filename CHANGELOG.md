## v1.0.0 - Product OS General Availability

Status: General Availability
Date: July 11, 2026

### Summary

Product OS v1.0.0 is the first official production release of the Product Leadership Platform. It establishes the canonical GA baseline for Product OS, the GitHub Product Leadership Hub, and the operating systems that support publishing, production, acquisition, and recruiter conversion.

Release decision: **GO WITH MINOR ISSUES**.

### Future Versioning Guidance

- `v1.x` releases should improve maturity without changing core architecture.
- `v1.1` should focus on launch polish, visual assets, analytics refinement, and public readiness gaps.
- `v1.2` should focus on repository maturity and reusable artifact depth.
- `v2.0` should be reserved for material changes to platform architecture, audience strategy, or flagship product model.
