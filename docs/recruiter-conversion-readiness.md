# Recruiter Conversion Readiness

Status: RC1 implementation checklist  
Last updated: July 2026

## Executive Summary

SPR-013.2 implements the highest-impact recruiter experience recommendations from the audit. The objective is to reduce time-to-conviction without redesigning Product OS or introducing new pages.

## Recruiter Time Budget

| Time Budget | Recruiter Question | Product OS Answer |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 5 seconds | Who is Saurabh? | Homepage and Executive Brief identify him as a Lead / Senior AI Product Manager across AI, platforms, SaaS, payments, and growth |
| 30 seconds | Why is he relevant? | Executive Brief and Professional Profile summarize role fit, domains, and outcomes |
| 2 minutes | What business impact has he created? | Executive Brief impact dashboard and Profile outcome cards show measurable results |
| 5 minutes | How does he think? | Product Leadership Briefs and Operating Principles show decision quality |
| 10 minutes | Should I interview him? | Role-fit briefs, GitHub artifacts, Profile, and Contact path provide interview confidence |
