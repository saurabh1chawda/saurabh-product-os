# Career Console Design Principles

## Operating Principles

- Operational work comes before reporting.
- The first screen must answer what to do next.
- Every warning must be deterministic.
- Every insight must cite supporting metrics.
- Read-only surfaces must avoid edit affordances.
- Density is acceptable when hierarchy remains clear.
- Text must be concise enough for repeated daily use.

## Visual Principles

- Minimal, professional, executive.
- Use restrained contrast and existing Product OS colors.
- Prefer compact tables and panels over marketing layouts.
- Avoid decorative backgrounds and unnecessary illustration.
- Preserve readable type sizes on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

## Product Principles

- Career OS consumes backend systems.
- Backend schemas remain stable.
- Private data is not rendered in public fixtures.
- Product OS remains the broader proof layer.
