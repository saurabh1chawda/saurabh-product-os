# Career OS Pilot Workflow Metrics

## Purpose

Workflow metrics measure whether the Career OS Pilot Workflow is reliable, efficient, and useful. Metrics are deterministic and directional.

| KPI | Definition | Calculation | Source | Target | Interpretation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Applications Qualified | Count of opportunities approved at Gate 1. | Qualified opportunities count. | Qualification artifacts. | Track. | Shows top-of-funnel pilot selectivity. |
| Qualification Rate | Percentage of opportunities that pass Gate 1. | Qualified opportunities / opportunities reviewed. | Opportunity intake and qualification artifacts. | Directional. | Low rates may indicate poor source quality. |
| Resume Generation Time | Time from approved JD Intelligence to assembled draft. | Draft completed time - Gate 2 approval time. | Resume Strategy and Assembly artifacts. | Track baseline. | High time may indicate component gaps. |
| QA Pass Rate | Percentage of resumes approved at first QA review. | First-pass QA approvals / QA reviews. | QA checklist. | Improve over pilot. | Low rate indicates assembly or strategy issues. |
| Submission Cycle Time | Time from opportunity intake to manual submission. | Submission time - intake time. | Intake and submission artifacts. | Track baseline. | Measures end-to-end operating speed. |
| Interview Rate | Applications reaching at least one substantive interview. | Substantive interviews / eligible submitted applications. | Application Registry. | Directional. | Display sample size next to every rate. |
| Recruiter Response Rate | Applications receiving explicit employer response. | Explicit responses / eligible submitted applications. | Application Registry. | Directional. | Do not infer from silence. |
| Interview Conversion | Applications reaching substantive interview after recruiter screen. | Substantive interviews / recruiter screens. | Application Registry. | Directional. | Indicates mid-funnel fit when sample matures. |
| Offer Rate | Offers divided by submitted applications with mature outcomes. | Offers / mature submitted applications. | Application Registry. | Directional. | Mature outcomes only. |
| Knowledge Capture Completion | Applications with pilot observation completed. | Completed observations / submitted applications. | Pilot observation artifacts. | 100%. | Required for product learning. |
| Pilot Completion | Whether pilot scope and reporting are complete. | Completed criteria / exit criteria. | Pilot charter, final pilot report. | 100% at closeout. | Determines readiness for next roadmap step. |

## Interpretation Rules

- Results from 10-20 applications are directional, not statistically conclusive.
- Every rate must show sample size.
- Immature applications must not be counted as terminal outcomes.
- Referral and source effects must be acknowledged.
- Resume variant comparisons require context.
