# JD Intelligence Template

## Purpose

Summarize deterministic JD Intelligence results before resume strategy is approved.

## Required Fields

- JD Intelligence ID: `[JDI-YYYY-###]`
- JD Snapshot ID: `[JD-YYYY-###]`
- Analysis date: `[YYYY-MM-DD]`
- Primary archetype: `[Archetype]`
- Secondary archetypes: `[Archetype list]`
- Stated seniority: `[Seniority]`
- Inferred seniority: `[Seniority with confidence]`
- Required competencies: `[List]`
- Preferred competencies: `[List]`
- Must-cover keywords: `[List]`
- Hidden hiring signals: `[Inferred signals with evidence]`
- Top achievement IDs: `[Achievement IDs]`
- Recommended Product OS asset: `[Asset]`
- Major gaps: `[P0 / P1 / P2 gaps]`
- Human review required: `[Yes]`

## Optional Fields

- Alternative archetype interpretation: `[Notes]`
- Ambiguities: `[Notes]`
- Unsupported keyword warnings: `[List]`

## Required Evidence

- JD Snapshot.
- JD Intelligence output.
- Traceable achievement and Product OS recommendations.

## Completion Checklist

- [ ] Classification is explainable.
- [ ] Keywords are evidence-backed.
- [ ] Gaps are honest.
- [ ] Simulations are not treated as production experience.
- [ ] Gate 2 input is ready.
