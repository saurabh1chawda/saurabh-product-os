## Measurement Observations

- Time-based metrics require disciplined timestamp capture. The templates capture dates clearly but do not uniformly capture start and end times.
- Funnel metrics are measurable but will be directional with 10-20 applications.
- Mature and immature outcomes are clearly distinguished in governance and metrics docs.
- Sample sizes must be displayed next to every rate.

## Validation Decision

PASS WITH OBSERVATIONS
