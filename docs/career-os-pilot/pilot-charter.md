# Career OS Pilot Charter

## Purpose

The Career OS pilot validates whether the frozen v1.0.0 pilot baseline works in real job-search operations across a controlled set of real opportunities.

The pilot validates:

- Operational usability.
- Workflow completeness.
- Data integrity.
- Privacy controls.
- Traceability.
- Decision usefulness.

The pilot is not intended to prove statistical superiority of a resume variant from a small sample. Results from 10-20 applications are directional signals, not conclusive statistical evidence.

## Pilot Scope

- Minimum submitted applications: 10.
- Preferred target: 15 submitted applications.
- Maximum initial pilot: 20 submitted applications.
- First review cohort: 5 submitted applications.
- Application period: approximately 2-4 weeks.
- Outcome observation period: up to 8 weeks after submission.

The pilot covers real opportunities only. A submitted application must have a JD Snapshot, Resume Snapshot, application record, submission date, and traceability to the relevant artifacts.

## Pilot Principles

- Real opportunities only.
- Evidence before automation.
- Deterministic analytics.
- Human approval for all external actions.
- Privacy by default.
- Exact JD-to-resume-to-application traceability.
- No inferred hiring outcomes.
- Stable software baseline.
- Operational value over feature expansion.
- Directional interpretation of small samples.

## Pilot Success Criteria

Detailed metric definitions and thresholds are maintained in [success-metrics.md](success-metrics.md).

Minimum high-level success criteria:

- 100% traceability for submitted applications.
- 100% recording of received employer outcomes.
- Zero committed private application data.
- Zero unresolved P0 defects.
- No record corruption.
- Registry and privacy validations pass.
- Median application-administration time below 10 minutes.
- Career OS replaces the need for an external application spreadsheet.
- Enough documented evidence exists to prioritize the next sprint.

## Pilot Exit Conditions

The pilot is complete when all of the following are true:

- At least 10 submitted applications have been managed in Career OS.
- The preferred target of 15 submitted applications has been attempted unless the Product Owner exits early for a documented reason.
- The first 5 submitted applications have passed the five-application review gate defined in [operating-procedures.md](operating-procedures.md#sop-17--five-application-review-gate).
- All received employer outcomes have been recorded.
- The outcome observation period has ended or the Product Owner has explicitly closed the pilot review.
- P0 defects are resolved or the pilot is stopped.
- Pilot metrics have been reviewed against [success-metrics.md](success-metrics.md).
- Roadmap decisions have been documented using observed evidence, not speculation.
