# Product OS v1.0

Status: General Availability
Version: v1.0.0
Release date: July 11, 2026
Deployment: `https://saurabh-product-os.vercel.app`

## Executive Summary

Product OS v1.0.0 is the first official General Availability release of Saurabh Chawda's Product Leadership Platform.

The release establishes Product OS as an executive, evidence-backed product leadership system for recruiters, hiring managers, product leaders, and AI product peers. It combines career context, product decision evidence, AI product frameworks, reusable artifacts, production engineering, analytics, and launch governance into one coherent public platform.

Release recommendation: GO WITH MINOR ISSUES.

## Vision

Product OS exists to answer one question faster and more credibly than a traditional portfolio:

> Would this person make high-quality product decisions in a real company?

The platform is designed to reduce recruiter time-to-conviction, help hiring managers evaluate product judgment, and make Saurabh's operating philosophy inspectable before an interview.

## Architecture

Product OS is a Next.js App Router application with static-first routes, structured data, reusable content components, global metadata, sitemap and robots routes, analytics instrumentation, and production release documentation.

Core layers:

- `app/`: public routes and metadata.
- `components/`: reusable interface and interaction components.
- `data/`: structured product content and Product Leadership Brief data.
- `lib/`: analytics and utility logic.
- `docs/`: release, governance, GitHub, publishing, acquisition, and QA documentation.
- `public/`: launch assets including favicon and Open Graph image.
