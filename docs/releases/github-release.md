# Product OS v1.0.0 General Availability

Product OS v1.0.0 is the first official General Availability release of Saurabh Chawda's Product Leadership Platform.

## Summary

This release turns Product OS into a production-ready executive evidence platform for evaluating product leadership, AI product judgment, decision quality, business impact, and reusable product operating systems.

It is designed for recruiters, hiring managers, product leaders, and AI product peers who want to understand how Saurabh thinks before the interview.

## Highlights

- Executive Briefing Center for a five-minute leadership overview.
- Professional Profile for career context and business outcomes.
- Product Leadership Briefs for decision-first case evidence.
- Decision Operating System for product judgment and trade-off thinking.
- AI Product Playbook and AI Product Studio for AI product evaluation.
- Product Leadership Operating Principles synthesizing patterns across briefs.
- Case Studies Hub organized around role-fit evidence.
- Contact center with recruiter conversion tracking.
- GitHub Product Leadership Hub strategy and release standards.
- Production SEO, accessibility, analytics, and performance hardening.

## Major Features

- Homepage positioning around product judgment before the interview.
- Executive Brief as the primary recruiter entry point.
- Guided visitor journeys.
- Product Leadership Brief framework.
- JoVE, Logix, and Simplilearn flagship briefs.
- AI Product Playbook architecture.
- AI Product Studio v1.
- Product Leadership Operating Principles.
- GitHub Product Leadership Hub documentation.
- Publishing, production, and acquisition systems.
- Release governance and public launch documentation.

## Breaking Changes

None.

This is the first official production release.

## Known Issues

- Microsoft Clarity third-party cookie diagnostics keep Lighthouse Best Practices below `100`.
- TBT remains above the strict `250ms` target on launch-critical pages.
- AI Product Playbook has a mobile overflow issue identified in final QA.
- GitHub repository metadata, pinned repositories, and social previews require manual GitHub configuration.
- Recruiter journey completion analytics requires production GA4 dashboard verification after deployment.
