## Architecture

Product OS v1.0.0 is a static-first Next.js App Router application. Public pages are designed as executive product review surfaces, backed by structured content and reusable components.

Architecture principles:

- Product OS is the canonical narrative and evidence layer.
- GitHub is the reusable artifact and implementation layer.
- Product Leadership Briefs are decision-first evidence.
- Executive Brief is the fastest recruiter entry point.
- Contact is the conversion endpoint.

## Analytics

Analytics support:

- GA4 through `NEXT_PUBLIC_GA_MEASUREMENT_ID`.
- Microsoft Clarity through `NEXT_PUBLIC_CLARITY_PROJECT_ID`.
- Lazy loading for GA4 external script and Clarity.
- Bounded analytics queue before GA initialization.
- Recruiter journey completion tracking for Contact email and LinkedIn actions.

Key events:

- `homepage_viewed`
- `executive_brief_viewed`
- `professional_profile_viewed`
- `plb_viewed`
- `plb_logix_viewed`
- `github_outbound_clicked`
- `contact_link_clicked`
- `recruiter_journey_completed`
