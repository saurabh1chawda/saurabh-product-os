# Audience Journeys

Status: RC1 journey model  
Last updated: July 2026

## Executive Summary

Audience journeys define how qualified visitors move from discovery to trust, evidence, and conversion.

Every journey should eventually lead to Product OS because Product OS is the canonical destination for product leadership evidence.

## Journey Model

```text
Discovery
  |
Trust
  |
Evidence
  |
Conversion
```

## Recruiters

Discovery:

- LinkedIn profile.
- LinkedIn post.
- GitHub profile.
- Referral.

Trust:

- Executive Brief.
- Professional Profile.
- Recruiter-friendly role-fit labels.

Evidence:

- Business Impact dashboard.
- Product Leadership Brief overview.
- GitHub profile for artifact credibility.

Conversion:

- Contact page.
- LinkedIn message.
- Email.

Best path:

```text
LinkedIn
  |
Executive Brief
  |
Professional Profile
  |
Role-fit Product Leadership Brief
  |
Contact
```

## Hiring Managers

Discovery:

- Recruiter share.
- LinkedIn.
- Executive Brief.
- Product OS link in application materials.

Trust:

- Executive Brief.
- Product Leadership Operating Principles.
- Professional Profile.

Evidence:

- Logix, JoVE, Simplilearn, or Mahindra Comviva brief depending on role need.
- AI Product Playbook for AI product judgment.

Conversion:

- Interview request.
- Follow-up discussion.
- Specific product judgment questions.

Best path:

```text
Executive Brief
  |
Operating Principles
  |
Role-fit Product Leadership Brief
  |
AI Product Playbook or GitHub artifact
  |
Interview conversation
```

## Product Managers

Discovery:

- LinkedIn insight.
- GitHub repository.
- Blog or newsletter.
- Workshop.

Trust:

- Clear README.
- Product OS context.

Evidence:

- AI Prioritization Framework.
- Product Discovery Toolkit.
- Decision Memo Template.
- Product Leadership Briefs.

Conversion:

- Repository star or fork.
- Newsletter subscription.
- Product OS return visit.
- Workshop interest.

Best path:

```text
LinkedIn / GitHub
  |
Reusable framework or template
  |
Product OS context
  |
Related Product Leadership Brief
```

## Journey Rules

- Recruiters need speed before depth.
- Hiring managers need decision evidence before frameworks.
- Product managers need reusable value before personal narrative.
- AI Product Managers need practical AI judgment before trend commentary.
- Engineering leaders need technical collaboration evidence before positioning.
- Founders need business outcomes before methodology.

## Operating Principle

Every audience should find the fastest path from curiosity to evidence.
