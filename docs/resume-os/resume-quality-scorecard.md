# Resume Quality Scorecard

## Executive Summary

Resume OS uses a 100-point quality scorecard to decide whether a resume is ready for submission.

Factual integrity is mandatory. A resume can score well in other areas and still fail if any unsupported claim remains.

## Scorecard

| Category | Points | What It Evaluates |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Factual Integrity | 20 | Titles, dates, metrics, scope, project status, claim accuracy |
| JD Relevance | 15 | Match to role requirements, competencies, and archetype |
| Business Impact | 10 | Strength of outcomes, metrics, and commercial/customer value |
| Role Positioning | 10 | Headline, summary, and role-fit clarity |
| ATS Compatibility | 10 | Parseability, standard sections, keyword coverage |
| Recruiter Scan | 10 | 10 to 15 second clarity and relevance |
| Hiring Manager Depth | 10 | Evidence of judgment, trade-offs, and leadership |
| Evidence Traceability | 5 | Claim-to-evidence mapping |
| Technical Credibility | 5 | Technical fluency without overclaiming |
| Formatting and Link QA | 5 | Two-page layout, rendering, and verified links |
| Total | 100 | Submission readiness |

## Release Gates

Minimum gates:

- Factual Integrity: 100% mandatory
- Overall score: 90 or higher
- ATS Compatibility: 90 or higher
- Recruiter Scan: 90 or higher
- Hiring Manager Depth: 85 or higher
- No unsupported claims
- No broken links
- Maximum two pages unless explicitly approved

## Scoring Guidance

| Score Band | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| 95 to 100 | Strong submission candidate |
| 90 to 94 | Submission-ready with minor improvements |
| 80 to 89 | Needs revision before submission |
| Below 80 | Not ready |
| Any factual failure | Automatic no-submit |

## P0 Issues

Launch blockers. Must be fixed before submission.

- Unsupported metric
- Incorrect employment date
- Inflated title
- Fabricated responsibility
- Broken required link
- Confidential information
- Prototype presented as production
- Company-specific project not clearly labeled
- Resume exceeds page limit without approval
- Contact information incorrect

## P1 Issues

Should fix before submission.

- Weak role-specific headline
- Generic summary
- Poor keyword coverage
- Strong evidence missing
- Product OS link used where a role-specific link would be stronger
- Bullet order does not match role priorities
- Skills section too broad
- Formatting issue that slows scanning

## P2 Issues

Future improvements.

- Better role-specific project could be created
- More precise Product OS link could be used
- Component performance should be measured over time
- Additional bullet variant could improve archetype fit
- Skills grouping could be cleaner

## Final Decision

| Decision | Criteria |
| --- | --- |
| Submit | All release gates met |
| Revise | No P0 issues, but score below threshold |
| Hold | Evidence gap or weak role fit |
| Reject application | Role fit too low or claims would require overreach |
