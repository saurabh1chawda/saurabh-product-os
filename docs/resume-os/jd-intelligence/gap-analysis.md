# Gap and Risk Analysis

## Purpose

Gap analysis identifies where the job description requires evidence that Resume OS does not currently support. The engine must never fabricate a mitigation.

## Gap Categories

- Missing required experience.
- Missing technical depth.
- Missing domain experience.
- Missing scale evidence.
- Missing leadership signal.
- Missing commercial ownership.
- Missing customer-discovery evidence.
- Missing AI production experience.
- Missing specific technology.
- Missing work authorization or location information.
- Seniority mismatch.
- Overqualification risk.
- Underqualification risk.
- Credibility risk.
- Interview defensibility risk.

## Severity Levels

| Severity | Meaning | Handling |
| --- | --- | --- |
| P0 | Launch-blocking for the application | Do not apply or apply only with explicit human approval |
| P1 | Meaningful risk | Mitigate honestly in resume, Product OS, or interview |
| P2 | Minor gap | Prepare interview explanation or deprioritize |

## Required Fields Per Gap

- Severity.
- JD source phrase.
- Current evidence.
- Gap explanation.
- Resume mitigation.
- Product OS mitigation.
- Interview mitigation.
- Cannot mitigate honestly.

## Mitigation Rules

Allowed mitigations:

- Use adjacent verified experience.
- Use Product OS as proof of thinking.
- Use a clearly labeled project, simulation, prototype, or case study.
- Address the gap in interview preparation.
- Deprioritize the application.

Forbidden mitigations:

- Inventing a metric.
- Claiming a simulation as production work.
- Changing a title.
- Inflating authority or team scope.
- Claiming an unstated technology.
- Hiding a required credential gap.

## Example

Gap:

- Severity: P1.
- JD source phrase: "Experience launching production GenAI features."
- Current evidence: AI frameworks, AI prioritization, Logix AI personalization evidence.
- Gap explanation: Resume OS has AI product and framework evidence, but not enough evidence to claim multiple production GenAI launches.
- Resume mitigation: Use AI product strategy and prioritization evidence; avoid overstating production GenAI ownership.
- Product OS mitigation: Link AI Product Playbook if relevant.
- Interview mitigation: Prepare a truthful explanation of AI product judgment and boundaries.
- Cannot mitigate honestly: Do not claim production GenAI launches if not supported.
