# Headline Narrative Rules

## Optimization Targets

- Mobile readability.
- LinkedIn readability.
- Resume scanability.
- ATS relevance.
- Executive clarity.

## Rules

- Keep to one line.
- Prefer 55 to 75 characters.
- Include primary archetype or current credible title.
- Include no more than three domain signals.
- Do not imply unverified domain ownership.

## Ranking Formula

Headline score =

- 30% role clarity
- 25% JD alignment
- 20% length discipline
- 15% differentiation
- 10% ATS keyword coverage
