# Resume OS Narrative Principles

## Operating Principle

The Narrative Layer improves communication, not evidence.

## Principles

- Preserve every fact.
- Preserve every metric.
- Preserve every evidence ID.
- Preserve chronology.
- Prefer clarity over cleverness.
- Make senior product judgment visible.
- Use plain executive language.
- Keep the recruiter scan fast.
- Make hiring-manager proof easy to inspect.
- Avoid hype, inflation, and keyword stuffing.

## Good Narrative Behavior

- Starts with role fit.
- Surfaces business impact early.
- Connects technical fluency to product outcomes.
- Uses varied but credible verbs.
- Keeps bullets parallel.
- Makes metrics visible.
- Uses Product OS as proof, not as a substitute for experience.

## Bad Narrative Behavior

- Adds unstated ownership.
- Upgrades adjacent experience into direct experience.
- Converts simulation into production work.
- Adds stronger metrics.
- Adds technologies not in evidence.
- Replaces evidence with adjectives.
