# Hiring Manager Narrative Rules

## What Hiring Managers Need

- Product judgment.
- Commercial thinking.
- Customer understanding.
- Decision quality.
- Technical fluency.
- Evidence of execution.
- Interview defensibility.

## Rules

- Connect business outcomes to product work.
- Preserve trade-off language where available.
- Keep technical claims explainable.
- Label adjacent evidence honestly.
- Use Product OS as supporting proof.
- Do not over-optimize for recruiter keywords at the expense of depth.
