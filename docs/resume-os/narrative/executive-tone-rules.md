# Executive Tone Rules

## Prefer

- Precise.
- Confident.
- Measured.
- Credible.
- Evidence-led.
- Commercially grounded.

## Avoid

- Marketing hype.
- Buzzword chains.
- Inflated leadership.
- AI buzzwords.
- Generic PM cliches.
- Claims that sound bigger than the evidence.

## Tone Check

A strong executive resume sentence should answer:

- What changed?
- Why did it matter?
- What evidence supports it?
- Would the candidate be comfortable defending it in an interview?
