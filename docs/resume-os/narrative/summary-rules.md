# Summary Narrative Rules

## Goals

- Make role fit clear in the first sentence.
- Surface business impact.
- Preserve technical credibility.
- Avoid unsupported AI claims.
- Keep length between 60 and 85 words.

## Deterministic Scoring

| Dimension | Points |
| --- | ---: |
| First sentence role fit | 20 |
| Metric density without clutter | 20 |
| Evidence specificity | 20 |
| Executive tone | 15 |
| AI/technical credibility | 15 |
| Length discipline | 10 |

Minimum target: 92.
