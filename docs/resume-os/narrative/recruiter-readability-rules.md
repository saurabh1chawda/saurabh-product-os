# Recruiter Readability Rules

## 5-Second Scan

The recruiter should see:

- Target role fit.
- Seniority.
- Most relevant domain.
- One visible business outcome.

## 15-Second Understanding

The recruiter should understand:

- Why this candidate maps to the JD.
- Which companies provide the strongest proof.
- Which metrics are most relevant.
- Whether there are risk signals.

## Deterministic Checks

- Headline under 75 characters preferred.
- Summary under 85 words.
- At least three visible metrics in the top half.
- No dense paragraph longer than 90 words.
- No more than two repeated bullet openings in a row.
