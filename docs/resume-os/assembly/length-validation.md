# Length and Density Validation

## Targets

- Total word count: 850-1,100 words for a two-page resume.
- Summary: 70-110 words.
- Bullet: maximum 35 words.
- Experience bullets: maximum 18.
- Skills: maximum 20.
- Links: maximum five primary links.
- Estimated page count: maximum two.

## Warnings

- Summary over 110 words.
- Bullet over 35 words.
- More than 5 bullets in one role.
- More than 18 total experience bullets.
- More than 20 skills.
- More than one large project section.
- More than five links.
- Estimated page count above 2.

## Estimate Model

The baseline estimator uses total word count, bullet count, section count, and link count. Exact page count is deferred to DOCX/PDF rendering QA.
