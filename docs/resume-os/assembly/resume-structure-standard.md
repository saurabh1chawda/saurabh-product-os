# Resume Structure Standard

## Default Two-Page Structure

1. Identity Header.
2. Target Headline.
3. Contact and Evidence Links.
4. Professional Summary.
5. Experience.
6. Optional Company-Specific Project or Product OS Proof.
7. Skills.
8. Tools.
9. Certifications.
10. Awards.
11. Publications.
12. Education.

## Section Priority

P0:

- Header.
- Summary.
- Experience.
- Skills.
- Education.

P1:

- Company-specific project.
- Product OS.
- Certifications.
- Awards.

P2:

- Publications.
- Detailed tools.
- Older early-career experience.

## Page-Budget Principles

- Maximum two pages.
- Current role receives the most space.
- Recent relevant roles are prioritized.
- Older roles are compressed.
- Early data role is omitted or reduced when not relevant.
- Product OS must not displace stronger production evidence.
- Company-specific projects must justify space.
- Remove repetition before removing high-value evidence.

## Optional Section Removal

Remove optional sections when they do not directly support the JD or when the page estimate exceeds two pages.
