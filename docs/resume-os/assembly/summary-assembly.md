# Summary Assembly

## Modular Structure

1. Role and years of experience.
2. Primary domain and archetype fit.
3. Two or three quantified proof points.
4. Relevant leadership or technical scope.
5. Optional Product OS differentiation sentence.
6. Target-role motivation only when useful.

## Rules

- 70-110 words.
- Maximum three major metrics.
- Avoid repeating exact bullets.
- Avoid generic adjectives.
- Avoid claiming missing domain depth.
- Do not overemphasize Head of Product scope for IC roles.
- Mention Product OS at most once.
- Use Product OS only when it strengthens differentiation.
- Use company-specific motivation sparingly.
- No long company praise.
- No copied JD language.
- No unsupported career narrative.

## Selection

The engine selects a summary direction from JD archetype, top achievements, gaps, and Product OS relevance, then composes a controlled draft for human review.
