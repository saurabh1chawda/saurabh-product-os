# Headline Selection

## Structure

Example:

`Lead Product Manager | Monetization, Growth & Platform Strategy`

## Rules

- Match target-role language where truthful.
- Do not inflate level.
- Do not copy the JD title when misleading.
- Use no more than three positioning themes.
- Prioritize the primary archetype.
- Include a secondary archetype only if material.
- Keep the headline under approximately 100 characters where feasible.
- Avoid generic terms such as visionary, dynamic, or results-driven.

## Output

Each recommendation returns:

- Recommended headline.
- Alternative headline.
- Evidence supporting both.
- Risks.
- Human-review note.
