# ATS Constraints

The assembly engine validates:

- Standard section headings.
- No tables in final resume content.
- No columns.
- No text boxes.
- No headers or footers containing critical data.
- No images or icons.
- No QR codes.
- No unusual Unicode bullets.
- No excessive special characters.
- Consistent date format.
- Consistent title/company order.
- Contact information readable as text.
- Hyperlinks have readable labels.
- Maximum two pages.
- Keyword coverage.
- No keyword stuffing.
- No invisible text.
- No unsupported abbreviations.
- Currency formatting consistency.

## Failure Handling

ATS P0 failures stop generation. P1 warnings require review before export. P2 issues can be resolved during formatting QA.
