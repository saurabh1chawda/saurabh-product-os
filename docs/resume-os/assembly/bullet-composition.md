# Bullet Composition Rules

Approved bullet variants exist, but assembly may make controlled edits.

## Permitted Edits

- Minor grammar normalization.
- Company or role terminology alignment.
- Removing redundant words.
- Shortening while preserving meaning.
- Reordering clauses for clarity.
- Changing `Rs.` to `₹` if formatting allows.
- Standardizing punctuation.

## Not Permitted

- Changing metrics.
- Changing timeframes.
- Adding unsupported technologies.
- Adding unverified business context.
- Combining unrelated achievements.
- Presenting inferred causality as verified.
- Adding production language to simulation work.
- Changing scope or authority.

## Required Preservation

Every assembled bullet must preserve:

- Achievement ID.
- Bullet ID.
- Canonical metric.
- Evidence source.
- Confidence status.
