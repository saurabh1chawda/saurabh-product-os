# Product OS Modules

## Purpose

Product OS modules are optional resume blocks that route hiring teams to public proof.

They should be used sparingly and only when they improve role fit.

## Modules

### POS-MOD-001: Product OS Proof Layer

Label: Product OS  
URL: https://saurabh-product-os.vercel.app  
Use when: The resume targets Senior PM, Lead PM, AI PM, or platform/product leadership roles.  
Supported skills: Product leadership, decision systems, evidence-backed product judgment.  
Do not use when: It displaces stronger work experience.

### POS-MOD-002: Executive Brief

Label: Executive Brief  
URL: https://saurabh-product-os.vercel.app/executive  
Use when: Recruiter or hiring-manager clarity is the main goal.  
Supported skills: Executive communication, business impact synthesis, career positioning.

### POS-MOD-003: AI Product Playbook

Label: AI Product Playbook  
URL: https://saurabh-product-os.vercel.app/ai-product-playbook  
Use when: The target role is AI PM, Generative AI PM, or AI platform PM.  
Supported skills: AI lifecycle, prioritization, trust, measurement, workflow design.

### POS-MOD-004: Product Leadership Brief

Label: Product Leadership Brief  
URL pattern: Use the most relevant case study URL.  
Use when: One employer story strongly supports the target role.  
Supported mappings:

- Logix: AI platforms and modernization.
- JoVE: Discovery and enterprise SaaS.
- Simplilearn: Growth and product-led growth.
- Mahindra Comviva: Payments and reliability.

### POS-MOD-005: GitHub Product Hub

Label: GitHub  
URL: https://github.com/saurabh1chawda  
Use when: The role values reusable artifacts, technical documentation, AI frameworks, or open product work.

## Module Selection Rule

Default header links:

```text
Email | Phone | LinkedIn | GitHub | Product OS
```

Default body rule:

Use either:

- One Product OS / GitHub proof module, or
- One company-specific project module.

Do not include both unless the role strongly benefits from additional proof-of-work.
