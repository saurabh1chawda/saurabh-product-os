# Skills Blocks

## Purpose

Skills blocks provide ATS-safe, role-specific groupings. They should be selected and ordered based on the target role archetype.

## Canonical Skill Groups

### SKL-PROD-001: Product Strategy

Product Strategy, Roadmap Prioritization, Product Discovery, Customer Research, Market Analysis, Competitive Analysis, PRDs, Go-To-Market, Product-Led Growth, Stakeholder Alignment.

### SKL-AI-001: AI Product Management

AI Product Strategy, AI Opportunity Assessment, AI Prioritization, AI/ML Personalization, RAG, LLM/GenAI Tooling, Trust Before Automation, AI Success Metrics, Workflow-to-Agent Thinking.

### SKL-GROWTH-001: Growth and Monetization

Monetization Strategy, Pricing and Packaging, Conversion Optimization, Funnel Analytics, Referral Programs, Loyalty and Rewards, Retention Strategy, A/B Testing, Experimentation.

### SKL-PLAT-001: Platform and Technical

Platform Modernization, API Strategy, DevOps Maturity, System Throughput, Release Velocity, SQL, Python, APIs, FastAPI, Redis, MySQL, MongoDB, Data Pipelines.

### SKL-PAY-001: Payments and Trust

Payments Reliability, Mobile Money, Transaction Workflows, Customer Trust, Risk-Aware Product Sequencing, Segmentation, Multichannel Integration, Enterprise Deployments.

### SKL-TOOLS-001: Tools

ProductPlan, Aha!, Google Sheets, Figma, Invision, Balsamiq, Miro, Proto.io, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Tableau, Hotjar, Optimizely, Firebase, Google Optimize, Jira, Confluence, Notion, Trello, Asana, ClickUp.

## Role-Specific Ordering

| Archetype | Recommended Order |
| --- | --- |
| AI PM | SKL-AI-001, SKL-PROD-001, SKL-PLAT-001, SKL-GROWTH-001 |
| Platform PM | SKL-PLAT-001, SKL-PROD-001, SKL-AI-001, SKL-PAY-001 |
| Growth PM | SKL-GROWTH-001, SKL-PROD-001, SKL-AI-001, SKL-TOOLS-001 |
| Monetization PM | SKL-GROWTH-001, SKL-PROD-001, SKL-PLAT-001 |
| Payments PM | SKL-PAY-001, SKL-PLAT-001, SKL-PROD-001 |
| Enterprise SaaS PM | SKL-PROD-001, SKL-PLAT-001, SKL-TOOLS-001 |

## Constraints

- Do not include tools or technologies that cannot be explained in an interview.
- Do not include AI terms unless supported by evidence or public artifacts.
- Do not overload the skills section with every possible keyword.
