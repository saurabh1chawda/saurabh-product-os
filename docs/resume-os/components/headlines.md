# Professional Headlines

## Purpose

Headlines position a resume for the target role without changing canonical facts.

## Rules

- One line maximum.
- Must match verified career evidence.
- May adapt to archetype and company context.
- Must not imply unverified authority, production AI deployment, or domain experience.

## What Can Change

- Domain emphasis.
- Role archetype words.
- Ordering of AI, platform, growth, monetization, SaaS, or payments.

## What Must Not Change

- Seniority beyond supported titles.
- Company names.
- Employment history.
- Production status.
