# Application Intelligence Dashboard Model

Last updated: 2026-07-17

## Purpose

The dashboard turns application records into an operating view for job-search decisions. It should help answer where Resume OS is working, where manual effort is high, and which role archetypes deserve more focus.

## Sections

### Pipeline

Shows counts by lifecycle state and flags stale applications.

### Conversion Funnel

Shows application-to-response, response-to-interview, interview-to-offer, and offer-to-acceptance conversion.

### Response Times

Shows average and median response time by company, source, archetype, and resume version.

### Applications

Lists application records with current stage, source, resume version, JD snapshot, and next action.

### Interview Rate

Compares interview rate by archetype, role pack, Product OS module, and source.

### Offer Rate

Tracks offer outcomes by role type, company type, and experiment variant.

### Role Distribution

Shows how the pipeline is distributed across AI PM, Platform, Payments, Growth, Marketplace, and Enterprise SaaS.

### Company Distribution

Shows companies, industries, sources, and funnel progress.

### Override Analytics

Shows most frequent override sections, average override time, and future automation opportunities.

### Resume Performance

Compares resume versions, headline variants, summary variants, project inclusion, and Product OS modules.

### Trend Analysis

Tracks response rate, interview rate, offer rate, and tailoring effort over time.

## Dashboard Rules

- Show synthetic data only in fixture mode.
- Do not expose private recruiter notes.
- Do not imply causality from small samples.
- Every insight must link back to recorded applications.
